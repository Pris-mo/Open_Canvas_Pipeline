
# **⚙️ Modify Your Course Model**

You can update the model’s behavior or the course materials it uses.

## **Update Model Settings (Prompt, Behavior, etc.)**

1. Go to **Workflows \-\> Models**.  
2. Find your course model.  
3. Click **Edit**.  
4. Update the prompt or settings as needed.

For detailed instructions, see:  
[https://docs.openwebui.com/features/workspace/models](https://docs.openwebui.com/features/workspace/models)

---

## **Update Course Knowledge (Files & Materials)**

1. Go to **Workflows \-\> Knowledge**.  
2. Select the knowledge base linked to your course.  
3. Click **Edit**.  
4. You can:  
   * Upload new files  
   * Delete outdated files  
   * Adjust knowledge settings

For detailed instructions, see:  
[https://docs.openwebui.com/features/workspace/knowledge/](https://docs.openwebui.com/features/workspace/knowledge/)
